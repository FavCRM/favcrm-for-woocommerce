FavCRM for WooCommerce is an extension that adds a loyalty program to your website, it allows customers to become members and earn redeemable rewards.

== Description ==

### What Can FavCRM for WooCommerce Do?
FavCRM enhances your online store by adding a comprehensive loyalty program. It seamlessly integrates with WooCommerce, allowing customers to become members and earn rewards that they can redeem for future purchases.

== Installation ==

1. **Install the FavCRM Plugin**: Download FavCRM for WordPress from the WordPress.org plugin directory or upload the files directly to your server.
1. **Activate the Plugin**: After installation, activate FavCRM for WordPress from the 'Plugins' menu in your WordPress dashboard.
1. **Access the FavCRM Page**: Navigate to the FavCRM page in the left menu of your dashboard.
1. **Create or Log In to Your Account**: Register a new account with FavCRM or log in using your existing credentials.
1. **Configure Your Loyalty Program**: Set up the settings for your loyalty program and start growing your customer engagement!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does the loyalty program work? =

Customers can register as members, earn points through purchases and engagement, and redeem those points for discounts or rewards on future transactions.

= Can I upgrade to a paid plan later? =

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time to access additional features and enhancements for your loyalty program.

= Is customer support available? =

Yes, we offer customer support via email and through our support forum. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or need assistance.

= Can I customize the loyalty program? =

Yes, you can customize various aspects of the loyalty program, including rewards, point earning rules, and member benefits to fit your brand and customer needs.

== External services ==

This plugin connects to an API to record user's order spending for rewarding points / stamps.

It sends the order data every time it created (If the user is not logged in, it will not be sent anyway).
This service is provided by "FavCRM": [terms of use](https://favcrm.io/terms), [privacy policy](https://favcrm.io/privacy).
